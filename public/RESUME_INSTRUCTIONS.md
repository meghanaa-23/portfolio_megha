# How to Add Your Resume

1. Save your resume as `resume.pdf`
2. Place it in the `public` folder (same location as this file)
3. The "Download Resume" button will automatically work

Your resume will be downloadable from the hero section of your portfolio.

## Alternative: Use Google Drive or Dropbox

If you prefer to host your resume elsewhere:
1. Upload your resume to Google Drive or Dropbox
2. Get a shareable link
3. Update the link in `src/App.jsx`:
   - Find: `href="/resume.pdf"`
   - Replace with: `href="YOUR_RESUME_LINK"`
